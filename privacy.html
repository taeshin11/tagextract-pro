Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TagExtract Pro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website located at tagextract-pro.vercel.app (the "Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

TagExtract Pro is a client-side web application that does not require account registration, login, or any form of personal information submission. You are not required to provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information to use our Service.

When you enter a YouTube video URL into our tool, this URL is sent directly from your browser to the YouTube Data API v3 (operated by Google) to retrieve publicly available video metadata. We do not intercept, log, or store these API requests on any server we operate.

1.2 Information Stored Locally

TagExtract Pro uses your browser's localStorage to save the following data on your device for your convenience:

  • Search History: The last 5 videos you extracted, including video title, thumbnail URL, and the YouTube URL you entered.
  • Tag Basket: Tags you have manually saved for later use.
  • Theme Preference: Your dark/light mode selection.
  • Language Preference: Your selected interface language.
  • Visitor Counter: An approximate page visit count stored locally.

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through the interface controls or by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

We may collect limited non-personally identifiable information automatically through third-party services, including:

  • Page view counts (via hits.seeyoufarm.com visitor counter)
  • General geographic region, browser type, and device category (via advertising partners' analytics)

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

2.1 First-Party Cookies

TagExtract Pro itself does not set any first-party cookies. All user preferences are stored using localStorage, not cookies.

2.2 Third-Party Cookies

Our Service uses third-party advertising and analytics services that may set cookies on your device. These include:

Google AdSense: We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Adsterra: We display advertisements served by Adsterra. Adsterra may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance. For more information about Adsterra's data practices, please visit their Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Service. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and traffic sources. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service's core functionality
  • To display relevant advertisements that support the free operation of our tool
  • To understand general usage patterns and improve the Service
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any personally identifiable information with third parties. The only data shared with third parties is:

  • YouTube video URLs sent to the YouTube Data API (Google) to fulfill your extraction requests
  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data collected by advertising partners as described in Section 2

5. Data Security

Because TagExtract Pro operates entirely client-side, your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on any server we control. We use HTTPS encryption for all connections to ensure data in transit is protected. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for cookie-based tracking
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, most of these rights can be exercised directly by clearing your browser's localStorage and cookies for this site.

8. International Data Transfers

Our Service is hosted on Vercel's global content delivery network. API requests to the YouTube Data API are processed by Google's servers, which may be located in various countries. By using our Service, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence.
